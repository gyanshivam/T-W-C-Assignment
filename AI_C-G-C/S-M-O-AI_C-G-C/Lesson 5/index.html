Indian Elephant

Type: Mammal (Elephas maximus indicus)

Lives in: Forests & grasslands of India, Nepal, Southeast Asia

Diet: Grass, bark, roots, leaves, fruits (up to 150 kg/day!)

🇮🇳 Cultural icon & ecosystem engineer – maintains forest corridors

Cool Fact: An elephant’s trunk has over 40,000 muscles and can lift up to 350 kg!
